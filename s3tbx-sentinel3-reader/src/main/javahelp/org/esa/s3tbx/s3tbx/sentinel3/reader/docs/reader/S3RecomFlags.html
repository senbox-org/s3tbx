Sentinel-3 Recommended Flags

SNAP generates for OLCI Level-2 data a set of recommended flags. Those flags are defined by an group of experts, named the Sentinel-3 Quality Working Group (S3-QWG). This group observes and analysis the data and suggests means to ESA and Eumetsat to maintain high quality data. It is suggested to consider these flags when working with Sentinel-3 Level-2 data.

For Land and for Water level-2 products different sets of flags have been defined. They combine already the existing LQSF/WQSF flags to a new flag. Where LQSF and WQSF stand for Land/Water Quality and Science Flags. The intention is to ensure good data quality when using/processing the Level-2 data. In SNAP they are displayed as masks in the Product Explorer and in the Mask manager.

Recommended Water Flags

The following recommend flags are defined for the Water products:
  • WQSF_REFLECTANCE_RECOM
  • WQSF_CHL_OC4ME_RECOM
  • WQSF_KD490_M07_RECOM
  • WQSF_PAR_RECOM
  • WQSF_W_AER_RECOM
  • WQSF_CHL_NN_RECOM
  • WQSF_TSM_NN_RECOM
  • WQSF_ADG443_NN_RECOM
  • WQSF_IWV_NN_RECOM
