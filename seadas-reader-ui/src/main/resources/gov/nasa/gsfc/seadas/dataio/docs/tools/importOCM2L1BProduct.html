NASA OCM2 Level-1B Product Reader

The Ocean Color Monitor 2 (OCM-2) instrument is a 8-band multi-spectral camera operating in the Visible ? Near IR spectral range. OCM-2 is aboard the Oceansat-2 spacecraft [https://www.isro.gov.in/Spacecraft/oceansat-2], the second in the series of Remote Sensing satellites launched by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) [https://www.isro.gov.in] and is dedicated to ocean research

This product reader supports the OCM-2 L1B products in HDF4 format

The reader may have poor performance when handling the internally compressed navigation data. Using the HDF4 utility hrepack [https://www.hdfgroup.org/products/hdf4_tools] to decompress the data, or re-compress with data chunking can significantly improve performance
